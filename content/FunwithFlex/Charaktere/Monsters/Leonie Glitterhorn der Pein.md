Medium Fey, Chaotic Good
Challenge Rating: 9 (5,000 XP)


---

Armor Class 17 (natural armor)
Hit Points 153 (18d8 + 72)
Speed 50 ft.

STR	DEX	CON	INT	WIS	CHA

18 (+4)	16 (+3)	18 (+4)	12 (+1)	15 (+2)	20 (+5)



---

Saving Throws Dex +7, Con +8, Wis +6, Cha +9
Skills Arcana +5, Insight +6, Performance +9
Damage Resistances psychic, radiant
Condition Immunities charmed, frightened
Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
Languages Sylvan, Common, telepathy 60 ft.


---

Traits

Innate Spellcasting (Glitterhorn).
Leonie’s spellcasting ability is Charisma (save DC 17). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

At will: Dancing Lights, Minor Illusion, Thaumaturgy

3/day each: Color Spray, Mirror Image, Misty Step, Dispel Magic

1/day each: Hypnotic Pattern, Dimension Door, Moonbeam (as radiant glitter), Glitterdust**


*Glitterdust (Homebrew): As Faerie Fire, but creatures also shed dim light in a 10-foot radius for 1 minute if they fail the save.


---

Radiant Glitterstep (Recharge 5–6).
Leonie teleports up to 60 ft. and emits a burst of radiant glitter in a 10-ft. radius. Each creature there must succeed on a DC 17 Con save or be blinded until the end of its next turn.

Magic Resistance.
Leonie has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Glittering Aura.
Allies within 10 ft. of Leonie have advantage on saving throws against being frightened or charmed.


---

Actions

Multiattack.
Leonie makes two attacks: one with her horn and one with her hooves.

Horn of Pein. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
Hit: 14 (2d8 + 5) piercing + 9 (2d8) radiant.

On a critical hit, the target is blinded until the end of its next turn.

Leonie can also force the target to make a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.


Hooves of Joyful Doom. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.
Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) bludgeoning.

If the target is prone, the attack deals an extra 7 (2d6) radiant damage.


Glitzerstoß (Recharge 5–6).
Leonie stomps the ground, sending out a shockwave of glitter in a 10-ft. radius.

Each creature must make a DC 17 Strength save or be knocked prone.

On a failure, they are also blinded until the end of their next turn.



---

Legendary Actions

Leonie can take 2 legendary actions, only at the end of other creatures’ turns.

Glitter Blink. Teleports up to 20 ft.

Dazzling Stare. One creature within 30 ft. must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom save or be charmed until the end of its next turn.

Glittering Wave (Costs 2 Actions). Leonie leaps forward; all enemies within 10 ft. must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity save or fall prone.



---

Flavor Text

> „Leonie, Horn der Glitterpein, galoppiert lachend über Schlachtfelder. Ihre Hufe hinterlassen funkelnde Abdrücke, die Helden zu Boden reißen und Gegner im Glanz des Chaos erblinden lassen. Niedlichkeit ist ihre Waffe – und Glitzer ihre Pein.“
